🥣 Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Frozen berries

Prep

Mix in jar. Refrigerate overnight. Grab and eat cold. No cooking.

🍗 Rotisserie Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

  • Store rotisserie chicken
  • Microwave rice pouches
  • Frozen mixed veggies
  • Soy sauce or teriyaki

Prep

Microwave rice (90 sec). Microwave veggies (3-4 min). Shred chicken. Mix. Add sauce. Done in 5 min.

🥄 Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • Frozen berries
  • Honey

Prep

Layer yogurt, granola, berries. Drizzle honey. No cooking. High protein.

🥤 Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Ice

Prep

Blend 30 seconds. Fast, filling, complete.

🥪 Turkey Wrap

Ingredients

  • Large tortilla
  • Sliced deli turkey
  • Pre-shredded cheese
  • Spinach leaves
  • Mustard or hummus

Prep

Layer ingredients on tortilla. Roll tight. No cooking. Pack for lunch.

🍝 Pasta + Sauce

Ingredients

  • Box pasta (any shape)
  • Jar marinara sauce
  • Frozen meatballs
  • Pre-shredded mozzarella

Prep

Boil pasta (8-10 min). Microwave meatballs (3 min). Heat sauce (2 min). Mix together. Add cheese.

🥚 Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • Pre-shredded cheese
  • Toast
  • Butter

Prep

Crack eggs in bowl. Whisk. Microwave 90 sec. Stir. Microwave 60 sec more. Add cheese. Toast bread while heating.

🥗 Bagged Salad

Ingredients

  • Pre-washed salad mix
  • Rotisserie chicken
  • Croutons
  • Bottled dressing
  • Pre-shredded cheese

Prep

Dump salad in bowl. Add toppings. Pour dressing. No cooking at all.
