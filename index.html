🍽️ Easy Meals
🍗 Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

  • Pre-cooked chicken
  • Microwave rice
  • Frozen broccoli
  • Salsa or hot sauce

Prep

Microwave rice and broccoli. Add chicken. Top with salsa or hot sauce.

🍳 Egg Bowl

Ingredients

  • Eggs or egg whites
  • Spinach
  • Low-fat cheese (optional)

Prep

Cook eggs. Add spinach while cooking. Add cheese if wanted.

🥄 Greek Yogurt Bowl

Ingredients

  • Nonfat Greek yogurt
  • Berries
  • Honey or sweetener (optional)

Prep

Mix together. Done.

🌯 Turkey Wraps

Ingredients

  • Lean ground turkey
  • Lettuce wraps or low-carb wraps
  • Salsa or mustard

Prep

Cook turkey. Add to wraps. Add sauce.

🥫 Tuna Snack

Ingredients

  • Canned tuna
  • Mustard or light mayo
  • Rice cakes or cucumber slices

Prep

Mix tuna with sauce. Eat with rice cakes or cucumbers.

🍤 Shrimp Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Frozen shrimp
  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce

Prep

Cook shrimp. Add vegetables. Add soy sauce.

🥣 Protein Oats

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Protein powder
  • Banana or berries

Prep

Microwave oats. Mix in protein powder. Add fruit.

🥗 Salad with Chicken

Ingredients

  • Bagged salad kit
  • Rotisserie chicken

Prep

Add salad to bowl. Add chicken. Use light dressing or half the packet.

🍽️ Easy Meal Recipes
🥣 Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Frozen berries

Prep

Mix in jar. Refrigerate overnight. Grab and eat cold. No cooking.

🍗 Rotisserie Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

  • Store rotisserie chicken
  • Microwave rice pouches
  • Frozen mixed veggies
  • Soy sauce or teriyaki

Prep

Microwave rice (90 sec). Microwave veggies (3-4 min). Shred chicken. Mix. Add sauce. Done in 5 min.

🥄 Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • Frozen berries
  • Honey

Prep

Layer yogurt, granola, berries. Drizzle honey. No cooking. High protein.

🥤 Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Ice

Prep

Blend 30 seconds. Fast, filling, complete.

🥪 Turkey Wrap

Ingredients

  • Large tortilla
  • Sliced deli turkey
  • Pre-shredded cheese
  • Spinach leaves
  • Mustard or hummus

Prep

Layer ingredients on tortilla. Roll tight. No cooking. Pack for lunch.

🍝 Pasta + Sauce

Ingredients

  • Box pasta (any shape)
  • Jar marinara sauce
  • Frozen meatballs
  • Pre-shredded mozzarella

Prep

Boil pasta (8-10 min). Microwave meatballs (3 min). Heat sauce (2 min). Mix together. Add cheese.

🥚 Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • Pre-shredded cheese
  • Toast
  • Butter

Prep

Crack eggs in bowl. Whisk. Microwave 90 sec. Stir. Microwave 60 sec more. Add cheese. Toast bread while heating.

🥗 Bagged Salad

Ingredients

  • Pre-washed salad mix
  • Rotisserie chicken
  • Croutons
  • Bottled dressing
  • Pre-shredded cheese

Prep

Dump salad in bowl. Add toppings. Pour dressing. No cooking at all.

🌯 Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas
  • Pre-shredded cheese
  • Optional: deli meat, canned beans

Prep

Layer cheese between tortillas. Microwave 60-90 sec until cheese melts. Cut into wedges.

🥫 Tuna + Crackers

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna (drained)
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • Crackers
  • Optional: hot sauce, pickle relish

Prep

Mix tuna + mayo. Scoop onto crackers. High protein, zero cooking.
