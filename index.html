🥣 Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Frozen berries

Prep

Mix in jar. Refrigerate overnight. Grab and eat cold. No cooking.

🍗 Rotisserie Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

  • Store rotisserie chicken (shred it)
  • Microwave rice pouches (90 sec)
  • Frozen mixed veggies (steam bag)
  • Soy sauce or teriyaki

Prep

Microwave rice (90 sec). Microwave veggies (3-4 min). Shred chicken. Mix. Add sauce. Done in 5 min.

🥄 Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • Frozen berries
  • Honey

Prep

Layer yogurt, granola, berries. Drizzle honey. No cooking. High protein.

🥤 Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • Ice

Prep

Blend 30-60 sec. Drink. ~600-800 cal. Perfect post-workout.

🌯 Frozen Burrito Bowl

Ingredients

  • Frozen burrito bowl (Trader Joe's, Evol)
  • Shredded cheese
  • Greek yogurt (sour cream sub)
  • Hot sauce

Prep

Microwave bowl (4-5 min). Top with cheese, yogurt, hot sauce. Done.

🥜 PB Banana Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 3 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • Honey

Prep

Spread PB on bread. Add banana slices. Drizzle honey. Close. Eat. No cooking.

🐟 Tuna + Crackers

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna (drained)
  • 1 tbsp mayo or Greek yogurt
  • Whole grain crackers
  • Hot sauce

Prep

Drain tuna. Mix with mayo/yogurt in can. Scoop onto crackers. Eat.

🍕 Frozen Pizza + Salad

Ingredients

  • Frozen pizza
  • Bag of pre-washed salad
  • Bottled dressing

Prep

Bake pizza (12-15 min at 400°F). While it bakes, dump salad in bowl with dressing. Eat.
